Causal Exploration: : :
The discovery of metastasis and pleural effusion are causally linked to the symptoms identified in the subsequent case. The dispersion of cancer cells in metastasis or the accumulation of surplus fluid in pleural effusion can result in alterations in the lungs and chest cavity, such as an increase in density, asymmetry, and other irregularities. These transformations can be detected through medical imaging and examination, assisting in the diagnosis and monitoring of these conditions.
