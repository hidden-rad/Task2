Causal Exploration: Despite the normal findings in the surrounding structures, the enlargement of the heart (cardiomegaly) can be attributed to a variety of causes such as high blood pressure, heart valve disease, or diseases of the heart muscle. The enlargement can lead to further complications if not addressed promptly. 

Therefore, the findings of A1~4 indicate the presence of cardiomegaly and necessitate further investigation to determine the underlying cause and appropriate treatment.
